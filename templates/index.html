Our Mission

Koulé Ingenium combines Koulé (the arts of building) with Ingenium (the science and tools needed for that). We believe every child, regardless of background, deserves access to quality education in emerging technologies.

Based in Ouagadougou, we provide robotics, AI, and Python courses to children from both stable and unstable backgrounds, creating equal educational opportunities for the next generation of African innovators.
